ABOUT

Introducing IN CAHOOTS, the 2018 Graduates of Graphic Design and Illustration at the University of Brighton.

As our degree show name suggests,
IN CAHOOTS describes how we interact with each other as a collective course;
we play, conspire, create, push, pull
and challenge the ever-changing boundaries of Graphic Design and Illustration. Our practises may range from building paper cities to computer generated landscapes, detailed oil paintings to documentary photography across countries. A haven of creativity buzzes beneath the ceiling of our studio 350, chaos and tranquillity somehow coexist comfortably in our space.

Our show aims to explore our
strengths as a collective as well as our unapologetic individualism. It explores how strong we can be when we bundle together, with the fusion of 150 different minds, backgrounds and practises,
in a show under one roof at the
Truman Brewery.

Be IN CAHOOTS with us.
